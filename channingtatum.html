About him

Channing Matthew Tatum was born on April 26 in the year 1980 and is currently 34 years of age. He was born in Cullman, Alabama. He is a dancer, a model, a actor, and a film producer. He got married to Jenna Dewan on July 11,2009 and had a daughter named Everly Tatum . He also struggled with attention deficit disorder, dislexia and alcohol problems.
